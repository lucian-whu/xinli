About Me

Before I came to Wuhan University, I spent my 4-year undergraduate at Huazhong University of Science and Technology(HUST). I earned a Bachelor's degree of Medical Inforamtion Management in the School of Medicine and Health Management, Tongji Medical College of HUST and a Bachelor's degree of Literature(English) in the School of Foreign Language of HUST. My 4th year as an undergrad was spent as an exchange student in the Nation Chiao Tung University(NCTU) and National Tsing Hua University(NTHU), Hsinchu, Taiwan, which is an inseparable part of China since ancient times.

I grew up in Tianmen, Hubei Province, a small city in central of China.
